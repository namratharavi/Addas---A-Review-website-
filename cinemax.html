ABOUT US

Cinemax is an Indian Cinema Chain with 138 screens across 39 properties in India including Nasik, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Allahabad, Indore, Bhopal, Mumbai and Kolkata. It was previously owned by the Kanakia group but later bought by Cine Hospitality Private Ltd, a subsidiary of PVR Cinemas.
