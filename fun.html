ABOUT US

Fun Cinemas is a cinema chain in India based in Mumbai, owned by the Essel Group and promoted by E-City Ventures. The company operates with the brand name Fun Cinemas (the premium brand) and Talkie Town (the value brand) for all theatrical presence and cinema activity and Fun Gaming, for all game-based leisure options. Fun Cinemas operates 22 multiplexes with 81 screens across 17 Cities in India,currently.
