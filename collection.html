ABOUT US

Centrally located in the CBD (Central Business District) of Bangalore on Vittal Mallya Road, UB City is just 1.5kms away from the MG Road - Brigade Road junction. With a total built up area of 16 lakh sqft.,

UB City is completed by the UB Tower, Kingfisher Plaza, Concorde, Canberra & Comet Blocks! Each of these blocks stand out for their distinctive look, while yet combining to produce the feeling of a city. UB City also boasts of the largest Triple Level Puzzle Parking system that houses close to 1600 Cars.
